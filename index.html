About Me

Born and raised in Brooklyn, New York, E did not realize her love for photography until later in her life. As a mother of 5 daughters, with little time to spare, photography was a fun way to express her creative talents. This hobby blossomed into a love of nature photography. Family trips and vacations took on a new meaning, as she saw them through the eyes of a photographer. No more did E leave the house without her trusted camera, lense, batteries, tripod, chargers etc. The walls of her home are now adorned with amazing photos of nature, taken on these very trips and vacations.
